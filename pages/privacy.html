Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not negotiable. Learn how CA Saathi protects your data at every level.

100% Offline • Zero Tracking • Complete Privacy

No Data Collection

No personal data is collected — CA Saathi tools do not ask for, store, or transmit any personal information. Your name, email, and documents remain entirely on your computer.

No cookies or tracking — This website does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or any form of tracking. Your browsing activity is private.

No third-party services — We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics platforms. Google Fonts is the only external resource loaded.

Offline-First Design

Tools run entirely offline — Once downloaded, all CA Saathi tools work without an internet connection. Your sensitive financial documents never leave your computer.

No cloud uploads — Your files are processed locally on your machine. We never upload documents to any server.

Client data stays client-side — All processing happens in your browser or on your desktop. Client confidentiality is maintained at all times.

Open Source & Transparent

Downloads from GitHub Releases — All executable files are downloaded directly from official GitHub Releases. You can verify the source and review the code.

Auditable code — The source code for all tools is publicly available. Security-conscious users can review and audit the code before use.

No hidden functionality — What you see is what you get. There are no hidden network calls, telemetry, or background processes.

Built for Professional Standards

Designed for CA professionals — We understand the confidentiality requirements of the auditing profession. Our tools are built with professional ethics in mind.

Client confidentiality respected — Tools handle financial data without ever exposing it to external parties. Perfect for audit engagements.
